HACKGT

HackGT is Georgia Tech's hub of hacker culture. In September of 2014, HackGT threw Georgia Tech's first ever national hackathon, called HackGT, which featured over 800 hackers, over 30 sponsors, and over $150,000 in prizes. Through events like this hackathon and other smaller hackathons, HackGT fosters a culture of expecting and achieving greatness in all walks of life.

My Work (Marketing Lead)

I manage the design process, website and content creation, and publicity of all HackGT events and programs. The main event proved to be one of the largest hackathons in the nation and the most active with over 30% more social engagement than any other hackathon, despite being only in its first year.
