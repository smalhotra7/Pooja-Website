ABOUT ME

My name is Pooja Vaidya and I am a fourth year student at Georgia Institute of Technology pursuing a major in Industrial and Systems Engineering, a minor in International Affairs, and a certificate in Marketing - and I’m passionate about all of those things. With my vast array of interests from statistics, teaching, writing, design, dance, and consumer research, I believe in being, above all, an artist. No matter what path I take I hope to combine both my technical and creative background to create art, tell a story and make a mark, both in and out of my career. Good music, history books, strawberries, juvenile jokes, painting, and wisdom from my youngest students complete my life.

DANCE

For the past 17 years I’ve trained in Kathak, a North Indian classical dance form, at Nritya Natya Kala Bharti (NNKB) Dance and Music Academy. Through the academy’s affiliation with Gandharva Mahavidyalaya University, I have been able to complete a Visharad Degree, a Bachelor of Arts equivalent, in Kathak, and am pursuing my Alankar Degree, or Masters, in the same. In addition to regularly performing with my peers, I’ve had the opportunity to teach at the academy for over four years. I am fascinated by how discipline in a classical art form reediness one’s physical, mental, emotional, and spiritual layers.
