LAASYA

Laasya is a traveling Indian classical dance competition that took place in Atlanta. Over 100 performers from all over the nation competed, making it one of the largest, most unique, and most popular Indian classical dance events in the southeast. Furthermore, all proceeds from the event were donated to Wellspring Atlanta which supports women's empowerment and victims of sex trafficking in the Atlanta area.

My Work (Logistics Chair)

I ensured that the show ran as smoothly as possible by fulfilling responsibilities ranging from venue bookings, equipment arrangements, show schedule, duty assignments, and stage management during the show.
