KHAYAAL

Khayaal is Georgia Tech's premier South Asian classical music festival. It aims to spread awareness of South Asian classical music to the campus and the area, and to promote youth talent within South Asian music.

My Work (Director of Marketing and Design)

In Khayaal's initial year, I worked on establishing the brand by overlooking the branding process, from conceptualizing the logo to establishing design and brand outlines and creating content through multiple mediums along with managing website design. I also managed the social media campaign, establishing Khayaal as the premier South Asian classical music festival in the Atlanta area and making it a successful, noteworthy, and unique event, even in its first year.
